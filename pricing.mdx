## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll only pay for what you use.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my rate limit?">
    If you exceed your rate limit, you'll receive a 429 error. You can either wait for the limit to reset or upgrade your plan for higher limits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?">
    Yes, we offer custom pricing for enterprise customers with specific requirements. Contact us for a quote.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a free tier?">
    We offer a free trial period for new customers. Contact us to learn more about trial options.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit cards and can arrange invoicing for enterprise customers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
